Benefits of Donating Blood
  • Less likely to suffer diseases
  • Donors have 88% less risk of heart attack and 33% less chance of cardiovascular disease.
  • Removes excess iron from the body, improving heart health.
  • Regular blood donation helps in reducing major health risks.
Facts About Blood
  • One unit of blood can save up to three lives.
  • Blood can be donated every 3 months; body recovers in 48 hours.
  • Blood makes up 8% of human body weight.
  • There are over 100,000 miles of blood vessels in the human body.
  • A red blood cell completes circulation in 30 seconds.
Before You Donate
  • Donation takes about 30 minutes.
  • Use this website to find a nearby blood bank.
  • Minimum weight should be 45kg; donor must be 18+.
  • Inform the staff about any health concerns before donating.
